DINAMULAG FESTIVAL

The Dinamulag Festival in Zambales is specifically celebrated in the town of Iba, the capital of Zambales province in the Philippines. It is an annual event held to honor the patron saint of farmers, Saint Augustine.
The festival primarily revolves around the abundance of sweet, juicy mangoes known locally as "Dinamulag." During the celebration, various activities take place, including street dancing, parades, agricultural fairs showcasing the region's agricultural produce, culinary competitions featuring mango-inspired dishes, beauty pageants, and cultural presentations.
Similar to other festivals in the Philippines, the Dinamulag Festival in Zambales serves as a way to promote tourism, agriculture, and local culture. It brings together residents and visitors to celebrate the richness of Zambales' agricultural heritage and the importance of mangoes in the local economy.
