Segara Suites

Nestled within Subic Bay Freeport Zone, Segara Suites blends contemporary elegance with Balinese-inspired design, offering chic rooms and suites as well as easy access to family-friendly attractions and activities. Discover the allure of Subic Bay at Segara Suites, located at Washington St, Subic Bay Freeport Zone, Zambales, promising pristine beaches and exciting water sports for relaxation and adventure enthusiasts alike.



Hidden Haven Subic Villa

Hidden Haven Subic Villa w/ Infinity Pool offers accommodation with a balcony, as well as an infinity pool and an indoor pool. With garden views, this accommodation features a patio. The villa offers rooms with air conditioning, free private parking and free WiFi. We are located at Pamatawan River Road, 2209 Subic, Philippines.




Mangrove Resort Hotel

Mangrove Resort Hotel was built in 2014 and in the foundation of providing a world-class luxurious escape from the ordinary. As mangroves serve as a protection for coastal seas, this resort has become a haven from the grind and hustle of city life. We are located at Baloy Long Beach Rd, Olongapo, Zambales.




Central Park Reef Resort

Central Park Reef Resort offers accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a restaurant and a bar. Among the facilities at this property are room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The resort has family rooms. We are located at 888 National Highway Barrio Barretto, Olongapo, Subic, 2200 Zambales.
