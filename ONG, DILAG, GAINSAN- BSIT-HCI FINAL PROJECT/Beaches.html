All Hands Beach

All Hands Beach, nestled between the strategic locations of Subic Bay International Airport and the Subic Container Terminal, is witnessing a transformative phase under the stewardship of Brighterday Subic Ltd., Inc. This idyllic coastal retreat is renowned for its crystal-clear waters that invite both tranquility and leisure. With its serene environment and the gentle lapping of calm waves, it stands as a pristine getaway destination.



Icove Beach Hotel

Looking for a place to stay in Olongapo? Then look no further than iCove Beach Hotel, a romantic small hotel that brings the best of Olongapo to your doorstep. The small hotel offers a 24 hour front desk, room service, and a coffee shop, to make your visit even more pleasant. The property also features a pool and free breakfast. Guests arriving by vehicle have access to free parking.



Subiza Beach Resort

Subiza Beach Resort is the right choice for visitors who are searching for a combination of charm, peace and quiet, and a convenient position from which to explore Subic Bay's emerald-blue waters and nature. It is an affordable and comfortable hotel, situated along the shorelines. The hotel staff offers an attentive, personalized service and are always available to offer any help to guests. Our 50 air-conditioned guest rooms from Standard and Deluxe categories, all the way up to Superior and Executive rooms offers the amenities of your own home.


Camayan Beach Resort

Experience pure serenity in our Sunset Suite at Camayan Beach Resort & Hotel! Unwind in luxury as you soak in breathtaking views of the bay and lush green forest. Let the vibrant colors of the sunset paint the sky as you indulge in tranquility and comfort. Your perfect escape awaits! We are located at PH, Ilanin Road, Camayan Wharf Rd, Subic Bay Freeport Zone, 2222.



By The Sea

Nestled along the pristine shores of Olongapo, "By the Sea Olongapo" is a tranquil haven where the gentle rhythm of the ocean soothes the soul. With its picturesque setting and panoramic views of the azure waters, this seaside retreat offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Guests can indulge in luxurious accommodations, elegantly designed to blend modern comforts with coastal charm. Whether lounging by the infinity pool, savoring delectable cuisine at the seaside restaurant, or strolling along the sandy beaches, every moment at "By the Sea Olongapo" is a blissful retreat into serenity.

El Molina Beach Resort

Elmolina Beach Resort is a hidden oasis in Barrio Barretto, favored by both tourists and locals from Olongapo and Zambales. It offers a tranquil tropical setting with tall palm trees, fresh air, calm waters, and native nipa huts available for day rental. The resort has over 20 air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities like comfortable beds, WiFi, and bathrooms with hot and cold showers. Visitors can enjoy water sports like Jet Skiing and kayaking, or play beach volleyball by the shore. The resort also accommodates team building.


Whiterock Beach Resort

Whiterock Waterpark and Beach Hotel features room service and a concierge, to help make your stay more enjoyable. The property also boasts a pool and free breakfast. If you are driving to Whiterock Waterpark and Beach Hotel, free parking is available. If you are interested in exploring Subic Bay Freeport Zone, check out a waterfall, such as El Kabayo Waterfalls. Whiterock Waterpark and Beach Hotel looks forward to welcoming you on your visit to Subic Bay Freeport Zone. We are located at Purok 3, Subic, 2209 Zambales.


Adventure Beach Waterpark

Adventure Beach Waterpark in Subic Bay is a must-visit aquatic playground for day-trippers, families, and friends. Start your day with a ride down the rapids at River Bend on custom-designed inflatables, then take on the Twin Twister Slide, a 3-story tower with two twisting slides that plunge into the pool below. Enjoy a glimpse of the vast ocean horizon as you climb. Keep the adrenaline going with the Splashing Seesaw, featuring various water slides, and relax in the wading pools under Rainbow Falls' gentle drizzle. End your day with a golden sunset on the beach.
